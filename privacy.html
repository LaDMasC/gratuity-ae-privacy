Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gratuity AE handles information. Gratuity AE is a local-first utility app for estimating UAE end-of-service gratuity for supported employment scenarios.

Information stored on your device

Gratuity AE stores calculation inputs, recent calculations, saved scenarios, and app settings locally on your device. This local data is used to reopen previous estimates and preserve your preferences.

Gratuity AE does not require an account and does not provide server sync for the core calculator experience.

Advertising

Gratuity AE uses Google AdMob to show ads. Google and its partners may process device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interactions, and related data to provide, measure, and improve advertising.

Learn more about how Google uses information from apps and sites that use its services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Sharing

If you use the share feature, Gratuity AE creates plain text summary content and sends it to the operating system share sheet. You choose where to send that content.

Data deletion

You can delete local app data by using the reset local data option inside the app settings, or by uninstalling the app from your device.

Legal and financial disclaimer

Gratuity AE provides estimates for planning purposes only. It does not provide legal, employment, or financial advice. Final entitlements should be confirmed with official UAE labour rules, employer records, contract terms, or qualified professional support.

Children

Gratuity AE is not directed to children and is intended for employment-related calculation use.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy, support, or policy questions, contact: support@uaegratuity.app.
